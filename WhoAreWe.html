Purpose

The primary goal of the research is to educate and empower individuals by providing insights into sustainable energy solutions.
By designing a web page with an application interface, it allows the user to upload their personal monthly energy bill. This will result into personalized information about the investment required for the installation of solar panels, the estimated payback time.
Key objectives include empowering decision-makers with insights into consumption, customizing solutions for Moldova's environment by facilitating financial planning.
Join us on this journey toward a sustainable energy future in Moldova.
