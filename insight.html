About the Study

The project is focusing on a two-year study to investigate the impact of solar panels on the energy consumption of a normal family home, located in the city of Nisporeni, in the Codri area of Moldova. This study is focused on a home that is 130 square meters in size and has a roof that has 70 square meters of solar capacity.

Data Collection Methodology

Our study involved meticulous data gathering, considering factors like seasonal variations, appliance usage, and the local climate.

Implications of the Study

By comparing these two distinct periods, we aim to highlight the potential benefits of solar panel integration in the given context. From energy efficiency to financial savings and reduced environmental impact, our findings will shed light on the holistic advantages that solar technology can offer to households in Moldova's central region.
